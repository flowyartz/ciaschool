Technology & Resource Fee
​All students are provided with original, state of the art learning resources, most of which are digital and internet based materials and subscriptions. The school’s ICT infrastructure has also been enhanced to facilitate the usage of digital materials including high-speed internet. Original books as well as manipulatives are provided for classroom use and remain the property of the school in order to adhere to the school’s mission of maintaining affordable tuition fees.

Uniform Fee
The fee of each uniform unit includes one set of school uniforms and one set of P.E. uniform. One set P.E. uniform consists of one shirt and one pair of shorts. For 30 USD the school uniform is tailored according to measurements of the student. Each student needs 2 sets of uniform every year. Dress other than provided uniforms is not permitted.

Fee for books and materials for Khmer Curriculum and Khmer Language and Culture
The set of Khmer books and materials include high quality, subject specific student work books; in addition to usual student learning materials of the Khmer curriculum recognized by MoEYS.

Advanced Placement (AP) for High School
Adopting AP courses will strengthen academic rigor and broaden post-secondary opportunities for CIA FIRST students who elect to take AP courses. Although the school will incur additional costs by implementing AP, CIA FIRST has made AP courses available for all academically qualified students without charging additional course fees, with the exception of those students who elect to take a year-end AP exam for which the fee is USD 200 per course. In addition, qualifying students who have elected to undertake the AP Capstone Diploma will incur a fee of USD 450 respectively for each of the AP seminar and AP research courses.
